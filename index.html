Who We Are

At InstaCure Wellness, we get it—life is busy. Who has time for long commutes, waiting rooms, and juggling schedules? Not you, and not us! That’s why we bring physiotherapy, chiropractic, massage therapy, acupuncture, yoga, and nutrition consultations right to you, on your time.

With services that stretch across the Greater Toronto Area, from Toronto and Scarborough to Brampton, Mississauga, Oakville, and beyond, InstaCure is there wherever you need us.

  • Physiotherapy
  • Chiropractic
  • Massage Therapy
  • Acupuncture
  • Yoga (In-Person & Online)
  • Nutrition Consultation

Payment & Billing Made Simple

At InstaCure Wellness, we aim to make every step of your healthcare journey as easy as possible— including the payment part. We offer direct billing for most major Extended Health Care (EHC) insurance plans, so you can focus on your treatment while we handle the paperwork. No benefits? No problem! You can always pay us directly, and we accept various payment methods for your convenience.
